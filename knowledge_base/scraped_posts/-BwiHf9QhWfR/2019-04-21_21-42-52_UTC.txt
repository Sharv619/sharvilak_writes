Sometimes when i miss you
I write to you 
Sometimes when i miss you 
I stalk your account
Sometimes when i miss you 
I read our old chats
Sometimes when i miss you 
I look at the moon 
Hoping you'd see sometime 
And know we share the same sky 
Sometimes when i miss you 
I talk to your pictures constantly
Sometimes when i miss you 
I cuddle my pillow so hard 
Until i shed a tear or two
I started roaming outside alone in the dark 
Hoping nobody to see me 
As i talk to myself about how life has turned amazing
Since Wednesday the 9th. 
I started taking long rides 
Just to be with my thoughts which is missing your Presence
I started growing up a bit daily
Just so one day i could make you feel proud.
Relationship for me was just a term for me 
Where society forced us saying it's necessary
But you showed me the bright side of loving unconditionally like father to his child or brother to his younger sister
You must be some kind of wizard because you change my thought process just in a snap.. Like Thanos 
You must be some kind of God's Person , because even giving you reasons that I'm not perfect
But you were there, holding my hands looking into my eyes and said 'if I was to find something perfect it wouldn't be love anymore it'd be just a relationship with no exciting things to know about others.' Love isn't about finding the perfect one and stick to it. Love is about finding the exact same weirdo as you who loves doing peculiar stuff which you love, make memes, roast you, cries with you, laughs at your silliest jokes, consoles you whenever you're down, cheer you up, motivate you through thick and thins and help you without reasons asked, and always keep you happy no matter what. 
If you have found the one who checks the above list, then please don't let them go, they're gem.
